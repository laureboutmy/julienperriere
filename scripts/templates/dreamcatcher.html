1Mission

Dreamcatcher is a social platform where anybody can anonymously recount their dreams. The community is all about having fun reading the most weird and creepy stories, and trying to find their meaning.

DreamCatcher is a school project at Hetic. We were asked to create a social platform using Wordpress. I worked on this with Laure Boutmy, Anthony Roux, Damien Rantoanison and Margot Bottet.

I was responsible for the whole artistic direction, and I designed all the screens, both the desktop and mobile versions. I enjoyed it a lot, I wanted to create a special atmosphere through a highly graphic interface, with a lot of tiny details.

2Website

I focused on creating a proper browsing and reading experience. This was quite a challenge, because the interface needed to have a lot of personnality and be easy to use at the same time.

Audiopost

Apart from the classic written posts, there are audio posts that the user can record using the Twilio API.

Community'sMilestones

Several types of special posts were imagined to bring visual rhythm to the feed, as well as providing insightful facts about the meaning of dreams and animate the community.

DreamsCharts

Sometimes, you just want the very best of something. So we created a chart section, showcasing the best dreams, nightmares and most commented posts.

Final design

3Mobile

Everybody wants to be able to scroll through other’s dreams on the go. obviously. That’s the reason why i created a responsive layout,
focusing on performance and readability.
