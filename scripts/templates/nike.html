1Mission

“Nike - Écris ton histoire” is a fictive project : an interactive video experience through which the user lives the everyday-life of an amateur runner, makes decisions for him by writing into his diary, and sees the immediate outcome.

This is a project we had to do within a two weeks long period, from finding the idea to shipping the final product. I proudly did it with Julien Acquaviva, Kévin Chapron, Jim Laurie, Lucie Reitz and Jérémy Zaloga.

I was responsible for all the design for this project, both UX and UI. Due to the really tight deadline, I had to deliver real quick and work closely with the front-end developer to make sure that the quality didn’t suffer the lack of time.

2Design

I had a lot of fun at every step of the process : from defining an effective framework to sweating the graphic details. I wanted the most discrete interface possible, and the video content to come first. But this was not only about video : i had to think of ways of making the whole experience truly responsive and immersive.

Storyprogression

Looking at the sticky bar, the user can appreciate his progress through the story and check out the chapters titles.

you'rethe hero

We wanted the experience to be about you, not anyone else. Users have their own profile, with their own info.

Sportsdata

A few of the data measured by the Nike Fuelband are shown in the profile. They are responsive to the progress of the story and the decisions made by the user.

Productplacement

Along with the sports data, the current shoes of the character are responsive to his level and are showcased in the profile.

Yourdiary

When the character has to make a crucial decision that will inflict on his future, his diary appears on the screen. The user is invited to a sentence, and our algorythm takes care of the following.
