1Mission

My Warner is a companion app available on both the ios & android platforms. alongside the powerful social features already there, this redesigned version brings the second screen experience with contents and interactions all in sync with your favorite warner bros movies.

Warner Bros France and Mfg Labs had already collaborated with the successful launch of the My Warner cross-platform ecosystem. They partnered once again to add the second screen feature to their mobile app, alongside a complete redesign, an Android and an iPad version.

I had the great luck during my internship at Mfg Labs to work on the design of My Warner. Under the direction of the great Martin Charpentier, I was responsible for the design of the whole Android application, as well as a few screens for the iPhone and the iPad.

2Movies

While watching a warner bros movie at home, simply fire the my warner app and it will automatically sync, bringing you awesome content at the right moment. our challenge was to find design patterns to make it fit every warner bros movies and that would invite the user to interact.

Celebritiesbest tweets

Here and there, expect to see one of your favorite celebrities’ feedback about the movie you’re watching.

Polls &quizzes

The landing page features a countdown to the exact time the episodes is viewable on the website, to make sure they don’t miss it.

Moviefacts

If you want to know every nerdy fact about the movie, you’ll love My Warner : discover everything about the characters, the story, the places...

Realtimestatistics

All in sync with your movie, realtime stats push your watching experience to a whole new level.

Final design

3Bonuses

It went through a lot of iterations to create the design of the player. the challenge was to find the perfect balance between an interface that’s easy to use and encourages interactions, and optimal watching conditions.

4Profile

with my warner, you get rewarded with points and badges for every action you make within the ecosystem. users can have an overview of all their badges in their profile, and check out their friends’ activity.

5Icon

we started working on the app before ios7 was publicly released, so we went through a lot of iterations to make sure it was consistent with the new design guidelines and wth the soul of warner bros and my warner.

6iPad

Alongside the android app and a few screens for the iphone, i had the chance to work on early designs for the ipad app. i wanted it to take full advantage of the beautiful and large screen. i worked on the home screen and all the screens of the movie sync feature.

Thank you for watching.
