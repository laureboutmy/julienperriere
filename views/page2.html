1Brief

Where Does My Tweet Go selects the most interesting tweets for any given subject, sorted by their “SpreadRank”, and then creates interactive visualizations of their journey across the Internet.

WDMTG is a in-house project by Mfg Labs. They had successfully launched a first version a few months earlier, and received tons of positive feedbacks. People wanted Mfg Labs to push the product further, it was time to create a Pro version of the service.

The week I started working on the design, WDMTG was awarded a FWA : the challenge was be even more fun to take on. Under the direction of Martin Charpentier, I had to design the new screens of the Pro version, and harmonize the existing pages.

Time to go

2Pro Sign-up

Customers enjoy complete freedom in terms of the quantity of data they want to pay for. This meant creating a higly clear and transparent sign-up interface, to make this process as enoyable and frictionless as possible.

A step-by-stepProcess

I chose to divide the registration in three distinct steps. This allows a better understanding of the final price, as well as explaining the several Pro settings along the way to the new customers.

Giving the userfull-control

Because the customers were meant to have complete control of their settings, I had to create interface elements that would create interactions.

Providing customers withvisual clues

Simple visualizations helps the users to get a clear idea of the pricing at a glance. Those are tiny details that create altogether a better experience.

3Custom feeds

Pro users are able to create feeds with their own settings, they can monitor the tweets on any keywords and twitter user. the challenge was to create a clear, yet very powerful interface to ease the administration and monitor the data consumption of these custom feeds.

Minimalistic interface,powerful tool

The necessary tools appear at the very right moment. I had to get rid of the technical complexity of the settings of the custom feeds to create a minimalistic interface.

Monitor & Organizeyour feeds

Users can create as many feeds as they want. That could mean : a lot ! As a consequence, the overview of these needed to feature the right amount of information in a very understandable way.

Need more fuel?Top up!

No reccuring cost means an hassle-free way to get more data to consume. At any moment, customers can top up their stockpile of tweets in the most easy and way possible.
